Currently in progress

Bachelor Thesis

My Bachelor's Thesis Searching Patterns in Simulated Network Attacks aims to create a modular test environment that simulates network attacks on webservices use the Metasploit Framework. The recorded traffic shall then be analyzed und visualized to discover repeating patterns in these attacks and if a classification into subgroups is possible.
The primary goal is to generate reproducible network attacks, along with their associated traffic and patterns, to further enhance research in this area.
